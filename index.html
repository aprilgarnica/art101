About Me

Poet. Writer. Singer. Filmmaker. Legend.
That’s what would be on my description if I was Jim Morrison, but I’m not…

My name is April Garnica and I’m a transfer student for Film and Digital Media at UC Santa Cruz. I earned an Associate’s Degree in Film, Television, and Media Arts at Southwestern College and now it’s just hanging on my wall. Studying close to the border was so amazing that I had two graduations: one in Chula Vista, CA, and one in Tijuana, BC. I love cats, ajolotes, music, nature, and films. It’s been almost a year since I’ve been saying this, but I like Blade Runner so much I dressed up as Pris for Halloween. I grew up and lived in Tijuana most of my life, but I recently discovered that I like Mexico City better… Anyway, I’m planning to incorporate my culture into my future films.
